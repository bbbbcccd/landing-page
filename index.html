Great table tennis players

  • In August 2017, Tomokazu Hariomoto, a Japanese table tennis player, beame the youngest ever winner of an ITTF World Tour men's singles title, winning the Czech Open title at the age of 14 years and 61 days.

  • Fan zhendong is a Chinese table tennis player who is currently (12 August 2022) ranked world No. 1 for men's singles by the International Table Tennis Federation (ITTF).

  • Ma Long, also known as "The Dictator" and "The Dragon", is widely regarded as the greatest table tennis player of all time. He held the ranking of number 1 for a total of 64 months, the most by any male in the history of table tennis.

  • Jan-Ove Waldner is a Swedish former table tennis player. He is often referred to as "the Mozart of Table Tennis". As of July 2021, he is the only person to win an Olympic table tennis gold medal representing a non-Asian country.
